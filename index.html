CU-EWB

Columbia University Engineers Without Borders

Founded in 2004, CU-EWB is a student chapter of Engineers Without Borders-USA working on sustainable engineering projects in Morocco, Ghana, and Uganda.

About Us

CU-EWB started in 2004 with the Ghana program in Obodon, Ghana. Since then our chapter has grown to well over 100 members with two more programs in Morocco and Uganda. Each program sends travel teams to their sites over winter and summer breaks. On campus our programs focus on project design, fundraising, PR, and travel logistics.

'Tis the season for giving! Please consider donating to our Year-End Fundraiser. Your donations will go directly towards helping the communities we work with in Morocco, Ghana, and Uganda.
