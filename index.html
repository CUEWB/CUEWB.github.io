Columbia University Engineers Without Borders

CU-EWB is a student chapter of Engineers Without Borders-USA, which is a national, non-profit humanitarian organization that supports community-driven sustainable engineering projects. Our chapter currently has projects in Morocco, Ghana, and Uganda.

Get Involved!

To get involved with our chapter, join one of our programs! For more information check out the program specific websites linked below.

Morocco

The Morocco program recently completed building a foot bridge in Ait Bayoud, Morocco. In addition to monitoring the bridge, we are starting a water distribution project in the nearby town of Izgouaren. Learn more about the Morocco program at morocco.cuewb.org.
